See when you’re free and plan holidays.

<b>At a glance.</b> View your vacation days in one of two layouts: a timeline or a calendar. Counters in the top bar show your vacation days for the current year — and how many are left.

<b>Sorted in seconds.</b> Tap the floating button to add new vacation days. Enter a name, swipe to select a date range, and you’re done.

<b>Switching phones?</b> Back up all your data on one device and restore it on another. Make periodic backups to keep your data safe.

<b>Night owls, rejoice.</b> A beautiful Dark Mode for late-night viewing. Toggle it automatically or have it follow the system appearance.
